Our Story

How It All Began

Dr. Fixit Mobile started with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and affordable device repairs to our community. Our journey began in Strathmore, Alberta, where we opened our first repair shop dedicated to helping people get their phones, iPads, and MacBooks back to working condition.

Within just one year, the demand for our quality service grew so much that we expanded to Calgary, opening our second location to serve even more customers across Alberta. This rapid growth is a testament to our commitment to customer satisfaction and quality repairs.

We understand how important your devices are to your daily life. Whether you need to stay connected with loved ones, manage your business, or simply enjoy your favorite content — a broken device can be incredibly frustrating. That is why we are committed to fast turnaround times without sacrificing quality.

Our certified technicians use premium parts and proven techniques to ensure every repair meets our high standards. We back all our work with a warranty because we stand behind the quality of our repairs.
